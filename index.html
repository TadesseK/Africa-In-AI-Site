The Data Divide

Today’s artificial intelligence is built on an illusion of being global. In reality, large language and vision models are trained on internet-scale datasets that reflect a single corner of the world.

They speak our languages poorly, fail to see our faces, and misunderstand our contexts. This is not a data gap—it is a form of **digital erasure**, reinforcing Western norms as a universal default and leaving a continent of 1.4 billion people behind.

Our Mission

Audit & Expose

We conduct rigorous audits of major AI models and datasets to document the systemic exclusion of African data, providing undeniable evidence of the digital divide.

Promote Data Sovereignty

We advocate for new standards in data collection and model development that prioritize data sovereignty, ethical inclusion, and benefit-sharing for African communities.

Build Awareness

We connect researchers, policymakers, and technologists with the resources, datasets, and partners needed to build a more equitable and representative AI ecosystem.
